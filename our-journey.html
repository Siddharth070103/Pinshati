Our Journey

The idea of Pinashti was born in 2021, during a time of global uncertainity. We saw a fractured fashion supply chain, limited opportunities for women and untapped potential across communities.

We envisioned a unified digital platform-one that connects buyers, manufracturers, artisans, fashion students and supply chain experts. A platform that doesn't just manage transactions, but builds relationship, encourages dignity and creates long-term impact

Most importantly, Pinashti was designed to empower women, who form the foundation of families and communities. Our journey is rooted in the belief that true fashion is inculsive, resilient and transformative.
